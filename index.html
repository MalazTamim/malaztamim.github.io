About Me

I am a master's student in Data Engineering and Analytics with a focus on Computer Vision at the Technical University of Munich (TUM), fully funded by DAAD.
I graduated from the American University of Beirut (AUB) in June 2022 with a B.S. in Computer Science, fully funded by the U.S.-Middle East Partnership Initiative (MEPI) through the Tomorrow's Leaders Program. I worked as a Research Assistant at AUB and TUM, as a Data Scientist at Infineon Technologies, and currently as a Computer Vision Research Engineer at Fraunhofer IKS.
My research interests are Computer Vision, Perception, 3D Scene Understanding, and 3D Generative Models.
